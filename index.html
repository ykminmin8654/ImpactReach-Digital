Our Impact

We found this organization through our own struggles and our peers struggling to find a place that could help build websites for non-profit organizations. Our poor peers weren’t able to find a place that was able to help them create a website in an efficient and proper way. So we decided to build a website where you can register for us to help you with your non profit organization.

We have worked with the Health and Wellness Organization to create a usable and easy website for their clients to use. It is very easy to manage and has all the information they could need. We have different pages for them to help them with contacting the organization for any further questions. For any patient we have reduced the need to take the time to drive to the hospital and back when they can find out what they need to know on the websites or on call through the websites’ easy “Call a doctor” site. This helps schedule times for calling a doctor, which helps reduce a patient from walking or taking long transportations to the hospital.

Our organization has also helped design the websites for Home and Mind; Home School Organization with making a usable website for students to easily navigate through the website and ask questions on what to do with their course and registrations. We were reported that there were problems with getting a way to communicate from the student to the teacher faster and vice versa. We created a direct chat log form teacher to student, which notifies the teacher when a student has a question or a notification for student when the teacher has a response. This has made students to teacher communication much easier and faster as well as registration for courses easier as well.

We also found out that many of our clients are non-profit organizations that have asked for website designs. Due to many people needing help with the website design we have decided to make an easy use website to go ask for help in designing a website.

Throughout the entirety of our journey, we have helped organizations like schools websites, hospitals websites, and etc. Through these we have helped benefit them through stuff like social media, website designing, and any webpage issues.
